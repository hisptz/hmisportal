HIV/AIDS Testing at ANC

The ANC HIV testing rate(% of Pregnant women tested for HIV) has increased from 84.9 % in 2015 to 95.3 in 2017. The awareness for HIV testing in the community has increased a lot. This is a significant change of more than 10% increase.

Tanzania national health portal
